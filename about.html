About

My name is Manjot Singh. I have Indian roots, grew up in Italy from age 3 to 15, and have been living in Canada for the past 3 years. The people I have met and the things that have happened in my life have shaped who I am — from the food I love (pizza, always) to the languages I speak and the way I see the world and people. I am 18 years old and still very much at the beginning of my journey.

These days my time is split between things I genuinely enjoy. I am currently working through a 10-week intensive programming project alongside two of my cousins. Outside of that I am teaching myself Japanese, a language I have always been drawn to and already had a strong foundation in. I also spend time sketching and staying active at the gym.

Before joining this accelerator, I was not starting from zero. In grade 11 and 12 I took computer programming classes and grade 11 was where things really clicked for me, I finished the year as the top student in my entire grade and was awarded a certificate for it. By the end of that year I had learned Python along with core programming concepts like booleans, conditionals, arrays, and functions, and built a simple fast food ordering app that brought all of it together. That foundation gave me the discipline and the technical mindset. After all, I will do my best... no, I will complete this accelerated course.

When I am not busy with everything mentioned above, I am usually watching anime or reading manga. Some of my favourites include Attack on Titan, Vinland Saga, and 20th Century Boys. I grew up playing soccer for about 8 years which taught me a lot about discipline and working as a team — values I carry into everything I do today. If you made it this far, thanks for taking the time to get to know me a little better.
