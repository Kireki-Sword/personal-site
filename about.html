About

My name is Manjot Singh. I have Indian roots, grew up in Italy from age 3 to 15, and have been living in Canada for the past 3 years. The people I have met and the things that have happened in my life have shaped who I am — from the food I love (pizza, always) to the languages I speak and the way I see the world and people. I am 18 years old and still very much at the beginning of my journey.
